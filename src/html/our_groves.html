Our Groves

The Mylonas Family olive groves nestle on slopes overlooking the Mirambello Valley, at an ideal elevation and distance from the sea. The mature olive trees, with their sturdy root systems, thrust deep into the rocky soil for mineral nutrients and moisture. They flourish in the cold, wet winters and hot dry summers, fanned by healthy sea breezes.
